How to Measure

Neck: Measure around the neck as you would for a collar.

Chest: Measure the chest girth at its widest point, all the way around, which is typically right behind the front legs

Length: Measure from the base of your pet's neck to the base of the tail.

Size Measurements

Extra Small: 12-18 inch chest

Small: 18-24 inch chest

Medium: 24-36 inch chest

Large: 36+ inch chest
